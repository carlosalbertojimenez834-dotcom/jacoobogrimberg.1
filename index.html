They Never Told You This

There’s a place where you live… even if you don’t know it.

A hidden space that knows you better than you know yourself.

It watches. It guides. It shapes.

That place is called the subconscious.

Do you have any idea how powerful it truly is?

Have you ever wondered why some people seem to succeed effortlessly—despite having the same opportunities as you?

Or why you sometimes dislike someone without ever having spoken to them?

Or how the perfect comeback suddenly pops into your head… but only after you’ve already left the argument behind?

Are you really the one in control?

Or are you just the puppet of something deeper—something that lives inside of you?

Joseph Murphy asked himself these same questions.

Born in Ireland in 1898, he grew up in a devout Catholic family.

But while others recited prayers, he was diving into books on hypnosis, psychology, Buddhism, and quantum physics.

He wasn’t looking for faith—he was searching for truth.

So he left the church behind and traveled to the United States, where he studied the hidden powers of the mind.

It was there he wrote his most famous book: “The Power of Your Subconscious Mind.”

And he wasn’t speaking in metaphors.

“If you reprogram your subconscious mind, you can change absolutely everything in your life.”

To Murphy, your thoughts didn’t just shape your emotions.

They shaped your health, your body, your wealth, your relationships—

Your entire reality.

He shared countless stories.

Like the man who healed from cancer by repeating positive affirmations every day.

Or the young girl who stuttered—until her mother whispered into her ear while she slept, “You have a strong, clear voice.”

Soon after, she stopped stuttering entirely.

Then there’s Dr. Joe Dispenza.

He was hit by a truck while riding his bike. His spine was shattered.

Doctors said he’d never walk again unless he underwent a risky surgery.

He refused.

Armed with a deep understanding of neuroscience, he turned inward.

Using meditation and visualization, he imagined his spine healing itself.

And it did.

He walked again.

So… what if we can alter reality using only the mind?

Murphy believed it.

But he wasn’t the only one.

Jacobo Grinberg, a Mexican neurophysiologist, took it further.

He studied Pachita, a healer from Chihuahua who performed surgeries using nothing but a rusty knife—without anesthesia.

And yet, her patients recovered.

Fully.

Grinberg wasn’t dismissive. He studied her with scientific rigor.

And what he concluded was even more mind-bending than Murphy’s claims:

“We live inside a fabricated reality. An illusion. A malleable hologram.”

He called it the Syntergic Theory.

According to him, if you can shift your consciousness…

You can shift your entire reality.

You become the sculptor of your world.

But soon after publishing his work to the public…

Jacobo Grinberg vanished.

No body. No trail. No clues.

As if reality itself swallowed him whole.

And this isn’t just the rambling of fringe scientists.

Even the Bible, in Romans 4:17, says:

“God calls things that are not, as though they were.”

What if that divine power… is also within you?

What if your beliefs, your emotions, your visions—

Are constantly shaping the world around you?

Maybe your reality isn’t as solid as they told you.

Maybe, just maybe…

You are far more powerful than you’ve been led to believe.
